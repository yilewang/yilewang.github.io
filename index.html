About Me

My name is Yile (Wayne) Wang. Currently I am a Master student of Applied Cognition and Neuroscience Program in The University of Texas at Dallas, working at Brain Circuits Laboratory (PI: Dr. Ana Solodkin). Our lab is using TheVirtualBrain (TVB) to do series of studies on clinical populations, including Alzheimer's, MCI, Parkinson's, etc. I do computational neuroscience research on brain dynamics, brain connectivity and brain simulation within TVB framework. Personally, I am interested in large-scale brain networks dynamics and its association with human's behavior and cognition. To know more at the about page.
