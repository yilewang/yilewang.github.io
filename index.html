    About Me

    My name is Yile (Wayne) Wang. Currently I am a PhD candidate of Cognition and Neuroscience Program in The University of Texas at Dallas. My research interest is on Computational Neuroscience, NeuroAI, and Psychiatric Disorder. My previous research experiences focused on using a computational modeling tool called "TheVirtualBrain" (TVB) to do series of studies on clinical populations, including Alzheimer's Disease. Personally, I am broadly interested statistical learning, neural networks, and the computation process in the brain.
